About

With more than a decade of Web Design, Development and Marketing experience, I'm confident that I will be a valuable addition to your organization.

In 2007 I shifted my career away from engineering and began a freelance web design company. Since then I have been assisting small businesses and community organizations with websites, graphics, marketing campaigns and communications strategy. I have organized hundreds of events including Technology Conferences, Spiritual Retreats, and 3D-Printing Classes. A few years ago I allowed my community-based entrepreneurial spirit to shift into overdrive and became the leading force behind Confluent Space, a 501(c)3 non-profit makerspace. I have also helped to develop DrewBoy Creative art gallery and Fuse Coworking space along with a number of lesser known projects such as Puncturevine Magazine. More recently I have focused my efforts on projects, organizations and businesses that support our mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being.

I look forward to working with your team! Thank you for your time and consideration.
